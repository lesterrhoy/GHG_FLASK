What is the role of Central Sustainable Development?

+

The role of CSD is to monitor and track the emissions progress at each campus within BatState-U TNEU. It provides diverse functionalities and valuable insights into consumption patterns, aiding strategic decision-making to enhance sustainability.

What is the purpose of Dashboard?

+

The purpose of the dashboard is to monitor and track the progress of each campus based on consumption, with filters for campuses and years. It also includes the total emissions, total number of records, current emissions for each type of consumption, and the number of trees to be planted for electricity consumption, helping to determine how many trees need to be planted for BatState-U TNEU.

What is the purpose of Report?

+

The purpose of the report is to manage consumption data by providing filtering options and allowing export in PDF, Excel, and CSV formats.

What is the purpose of Forecast Analytics?

+

The purpose of forecast analytics is to identify consumption trends and gaps on a yearly and monthly basis using the FB Prophet forecast model. It presents actual and forecasted data, offering valuable insights for implementing preventive measures to enhance the university's sustainability efforts.

What is the purpose of Manage Account?

+

The purpose of "Manage Account" is to create accounts for the Sustainable Development Office (SDO) of each campus, which is responsible for managing three user roles: Environmental Management Unit, Procurement Office, and External Affairs. The CSD also has access to save and delete the accounts they create.
