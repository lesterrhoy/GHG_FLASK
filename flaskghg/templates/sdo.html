What is the role of Sustainable Development Office?

+

The role of SDO is to monitor and track the emissions progress of campus within BatState-U TNEU. It provides diverse functionalities and valuable insights into consumption patterns, aiding strategic decision-making to enhance sustainability.

What is the purpose of Dashboard?

+

The purpose of the dashboard is to monitor and track the progress of the campus based on consumption, with filters for years. It also includes the total emissions, current emissions for each type of consumption of the campus.

What is the purpose of Report?

+

The purpose of the report is to manage consumption data by providing filtering options and allowing export in PDF, Excel, and CSV formats.

What is the purpose of Forecast Analytics?

+

The purpose of forecast analytics is to identify consumption trends and gaps on a yearly and monthly basis using the FB Prophet forecast model. It presents actual and forecasted data, offering valuable insights for implementing preventive measures to enhance the university's sustainability efforts.

What is the purpose of Manage Account?

+

The purpose of "Manage Account" is to create accounts for the Offices (SDO) of each campus, which is SDO is responsible for managing three user roles: Environmental Management Unit, Procurement Office, and External Affairs. The SDO also has access to save and delete the accounts they create.
